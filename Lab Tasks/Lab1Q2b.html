Story Competetion Guideline

In order to submit your story for the Monthly Haunted Story contest, you have to follow the guidelines below:

Stories can have the following categories:

  1. Novel.
  2. Short Story.
  3. Real Life Story.
  4. Articles.

Here are the basic guidelines:

  1. Basic Guidelines
    1. Story must be written within 1200 words.
    2. Computer Typed:
      1. Font-Size: 12 pt.
      2. Paragraph: Double-Line break.
      3. Font-Style: Times New Roman.
  2. Well-Formatted with a Cover Page.
  3. Cover Page Content:
    1. Story Name.
    2. Author's Name.
    3. Submission Date.
    4. Category.
